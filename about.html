About Chi'Va™

Addressing Customer Pain Points

Chi'Va™ is a a trademarked AI-powered platform designed to improve efficiency and outcomes in behavioral health care. It addresses critical challenges such as revenue retention and operational strain by integrating streamlined processes with evidence-based methodologies. Leveraging advanced AI/ML technology, Chi'Va enables healthcare network managers and providers to focus on delivering high-quality outcomes while achieving improved ROI.
