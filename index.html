Wellness and Performance Enablement

Chi'Va is an AI-guided, self-directed mental performance platform designed to empower individuals through neuroscience-backed and somatic techniques. Our mission is to reduce cognitive load and enable meaningful, lasting behavior change.

Mission & Vision

We believe in agency, empowerment, and accessibility. Chi'Va helps users take ownership of their nervous system—enabling self-led optimization, resilience, and sustainable high performance. We build empowerment architecture—not therapy apps.

Technology

Chi'Va delivers behaviorally informed tooling through a secure, AI-powered interface. Our platform incorporates decision architecture and structured protocols to guide users safely through sensitive emotional and cognitive states without requiring clinical supervision.

  • Agentic platforms supporting behavioral change
  • Cognitive load reduction tools
  • Somatic awareness and performance coaching
  • Guided mental clarity and stress regulation sessions

Compliance & Ethics

Chi'Va operates within regulated environments with a strong emphasis on user protection. We design tooling with behavioral compliance, consent management, and user dignity at the center. Our platform supports:

  • HIPAA-aligned data protection standards
  • Decision architecture design for sensitive domains
  • Transparent use policies and opt-in experiences
  • Audit trails and secure storage of user interaction data
