We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud storage, payment processors) who perform functions on our behalf. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and only use it for the purposes of providing services to us.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, in response to a valid subpoena, or to protect our rights or the rights of others.
